GULAB JAMUN

Jamuns
Mix 3/4 cup milk powder, half cup maida and half tsp baking powder
Add 2tbsp ghee - Mix
Add Milk - Mix to form a soft dough
Do not knead
Cover rest for 10-15 minutes

Sugar Syrup
In a sauce pan add the following:
2 cup sugar 
2 cup water 
2 cardamon 
1/4 tsp saffron

Boil for 5 minutes until syrup is sticky
Turn off - add 1 tsp lemon juice
cover and keep aside

Prepare small balls from dough - no cracks
deep fry in medium hot oil
stir continuously until golden brown
put jamuns in hot sugar syrup
Rest for 2 hours
ENJOY :)
