BHINDI Recipe

Cut Bhindi/Okra in roundels
Cut Onion length wise
Cut Tomatos diced

Heat Wok
Add Oil 2 Tbsp
Add mustard (let splutter)
Add Jeera, turmeric, asefotida
Add Onion - Fry till golden
Add Tomatoes - until mushy
Add Bhindi
Saute for 5-7 minutes
Add salt, chilli powder, jeera powder, amchur powder
Cook for 2 more minutes uncovered
Enjoy
