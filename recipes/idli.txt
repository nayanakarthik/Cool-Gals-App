IDLI Recipe

Soak the following for 6 hours
Half cup udad dal
Fenugreek seeds

Soak the following for 6 hours in another bowl
One cup rice 

Soak Poha for 30 minutes
2tbsp poha

Blend Udad until thick and frothy
Grind smooth consistency - Add water as needed - Batter should be medium consistency not too thick not too thin
Mix with hands

Set aside for fermenting for 12 hours
Batter doubles in volume

Grease Idli plates
Pour batter
Steam for 15 minutes with no whistle
gently remove soft idlis
Enjoy
